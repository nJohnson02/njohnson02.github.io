Welcome to LivePond.net!

Live webcam of a relaxing koi pond in Boise, Idaho.  This pond contains 2 koi and several goldfish who have been in the the pond for almost two years.  The yellow and black spotted fish, and the black, white, and orange spotted fish are koi.  All the other fish are normal goldfish.

Best Viewing times:  2:30pm to 5:30pm
Normal Feeding time:  3:00pm to 6:30pm
