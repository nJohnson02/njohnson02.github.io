About

Dapp3r Craft is an online survival multiplayer server, similar to hermitcraft. The server is pretty chill and we have a good time. We like to build our bases far apart and shops at spawn connected through the nether. We are always looking for new members, so if you want to play, just hop on!

Rules

  1. No griefing bases, farms, or other structures
  2. Don't steal items from players or farms
  3. Try to avoid swearing in chat
  4. Hacking is not allowed without permission from an admin
  5. Do not build in the spawn region without permission
  6. If you blow up a creeper, you have to fix the damage
  7. Be nice to other players and admins
  8. Admins reserve the right to ban violators of these rules
